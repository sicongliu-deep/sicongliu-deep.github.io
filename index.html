Sicong Liu

I'm currently an associate professor in the School of Computer Science at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). I work in the CrowdHMI Lab of NPU, under the leadership of Prof. Zhiwen Yu. And I'm also doing two-years postdoc at NWPU, supervised by Prof. Bin Guo on deep learning in AIoT, crowd computing for Human-Machine-IoT. Before joining NPU, I received my Ph.D. degree from Xidian University, China, in 2020, supervied by Prof. Junzhao Du. And I had an experience as a joint PhD. student at Rice University from 2017 to 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Lin Zhong.

Research:

I care about the knowledge discoveries towards building ubiquitous intelligent system for benefiting common users and reducing the workload of non-expert developers.
  • On-demand and Automated Deep Learning Model.
  • Spatio-temporal Data Mining and Graph Neural Network.
  • Crowd Computing for Human-Machine-IoT.
  • Deep Learning Algorithm and System Co-design.
