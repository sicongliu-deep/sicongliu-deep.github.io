Yang Liu

I received the B.E. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University, China, in 2016 and the Ph.D. degree from City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong in 2020. I am currently a Postdoc working with Dr. Zhenjiang Li in the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong. I am working on developing intelligent mobile and wearable sensing technologies from IoT systems, machine learning and cyber security.

Recent News:

  • I am going on the job market from 10/2021.
  • Our recent work Mobile Phones Know Your Keystrokes through the Sounds from Finger's Tapping on the Screen is accepted at ICDCS 2020.
  • Our recent work Adversarial Attacks and Defenses on Cyber-Physical Systems: A Survey is accepted to IEEE Internet of Things Journal 2020.
  • Our recent work cDeepArch: A Compact Deep Neural Network Architecture for Mobile Sensing is accepted to IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking 2019.
  • I presented our work Real-time Arm Skeleton Tracking and Gesture Inference Tolerant to Missing Wearable Sensors at MobiSys 2019.
